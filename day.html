Islamic Daily Routine


Thahajjud-Wake up early, perform ablution (Wudu), and pray.
Fajr (Dawn Prayer) - Pray Fajr
Morning Adhkar - Recite morning duas and seek Allah’s protection.
Qur’an Recitation - Read and reflect on the Qur’an.
Work/Study - Start with the intention of seeking knowledge.
Dhuhr (Noon Prayer) - Take a break from work to pray.
Asr (Afternoon Prayer) - Perform Asr prayer and engage in good deeds.
Maghrib (Sunset Prayer) - Pray Maghrib and make evening supplications.
Isha (Night Prayer) - Pray Isha and reflect on your day.
Night Supplications - Recite evening duas before sleeping.
